About

Cave Miner: Clicker Game invites you deep underground into a world of infinite riches, danger, and discovery. Armed with only your pickaxe (and later, high-tech drills and helpers), you’ll tap your way through rock, stone, and rare ore veins to become the richest miner in history.

This idle clicker combines satisfying tap mechanics with upgrade depth and layered progression. Whether you’re actively mining or earning gold while offline, every dig brings you closer to becoming a legendary cave tycoon.
